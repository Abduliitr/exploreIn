What is Product Management?


Product Management can have different meanings to different people based on their understanding and perspective and the working sector they want to get into. To me, Product Management is all about understanding the sentiment of the users, whether they are the customers or employees from different teams, and coming up with solutions to every problem in a way that benefits all the concerned stakeholders.
